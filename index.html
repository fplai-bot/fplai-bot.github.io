About FPL.ai

Your one-stop shop for all the FPL statistics you could ever want, plus many more fun and informative features.

At the core of the bot is detailed information about every player in the Premier League, giving you access to:

  • Underlying stats including xG, shots, and key passes
  • Reviews and analysis of any past match
  • Point predictions for future matches that are constantly improving
  • The ability to suggest optimal transfers for your FPL team

That's not all. FPL.ai also allows you to check live bonus points during a match directly through Discord, keep track of injured players, see which players are moving the market, and even simulate matches between any two clubs. There's so much to explore - and, best of all, it's free and always will be.

A Bit About Me... just for fun

I'm a data science student based in California. My FPL experience is not much to write home about - I only started passionately playing in 2018 - but I've quickly grown to love the game. I've also always been somewhat of a hardcore programmer, and during the COVID lockdowns of 2020 I was inspired to combine my two interests. FPL.ai has been in development since May 2020; by August, it entered the Beta phase and began to appear on a couple FPL Discord servers. Ever since then, I've been adding new features nonstop, and the bot officially released to the public in December. The bot is now a part of over 350 servers and this figure is growing nonstop.
