Bonus Games

At the core of the bot is detailed information about every player in the Premier League, giving you access to:

  • /wordle
  • Play a game of Wordle where the goal is to identify a Premier Legue player.
  • /tictactoe
  • Play a game of Premier League Tic-Tac-Toe, based on the popular online game. Can be played against the bot or another user.
  • /coin
  • Indecisive? Flip a coin!
  • /dice
  • A coin but with more sides.
